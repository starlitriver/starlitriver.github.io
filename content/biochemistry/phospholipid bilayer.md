a phospholipid bilayer is a structure in biology known for constituting permeable and semi-permeable [[membrane|membranes]]

a phospholipid bilayer is made of two layers of [[lipid|lipids]], forming two sheets where the hydrophilic heads form the outside of the membrane and the hydrophobic tails form the middle of the membrane
