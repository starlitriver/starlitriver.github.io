a plasma membrane is a network of lipids and proteins based upon a [[phospholipid bilayer]]
the [[cell membrane]] is an example of a plasma membrane

a plasma membrane consists of primarily phospholipids, cholesterol and proteins

the currently accepted model for the structure of the plasma membrane is called the fluid mosaic model

according to the fluid mosaic model, the plasma membrane is a mosaic of components that move freely and fluidly in the plane of the membrane

![[fluid mosaic model.jpg]]

this fluidity means that if you rupture any region of the plasma membrane, the membrane would flow like an ocean and return to its original shape

integral membranes that extend across the membrane through the other side are called protein channels or transmembrane proteins
