Prokaryotic cells replicate using binary fission.

Binary fission steps.
- DNA is replicated and attached to two sides of the cell membrane.
- The cell elongates.
- Split into two.
 
Things.
- Fast and easy.
- Less energy needed.
- Asexual reproduction.
- Decreased population variation.
