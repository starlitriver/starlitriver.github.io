in cell biology, a mitochondrion is an [[organelle]] found in most eukaryotes


mitochondria have a double membrane structure.

the mitochondria is the site of [[cellular respiration]], which produces ATP.



the number of mitochondria in a cell can very widely by organism, tissue, and cell type
