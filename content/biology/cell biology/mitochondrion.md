A mitochondrion is an [[organelle]] for [[cellular respiration]], so produces ATP.

Mitochondria are membrane bound organelles; they have a double membrane structure.

## cristae

The cristae is the folded membrane the bounds the matrix.

The folds of the cristae increases the surface area, allowing more places for the electron transport chain (where most ATP is produced) to be active.
