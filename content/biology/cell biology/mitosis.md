mitosis is a part of the [[eukaryotic cell cycle]] in which the replicated chromosomes are separated into two new nuclei

mitosis is preceded by [[DNA replication]], during which DNA replication occurs

stages of mitosis are
- [[prophase]]
- [[metaphase]]
- [[anaphase]]
- [[telophase]]
- [[cytokinesis]]
