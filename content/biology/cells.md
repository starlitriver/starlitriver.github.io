cells are the smallest structural and functional unit of an organism

all cells consists of [[cytoplasm]] enclosed within a [[cell membrane]]
and contains many [[macromolecules]] such as
[[proteins]] and [[deoxyribonucleic acid|DNA]] and [[ribonucleic acid|RNA]] 


different cells have different [[cell sizes]] but most plant and animal cells have dimensions between 1 and 100 micrometers

the two main types of cells are [[prokaryotes]] and [[eukaryotes]]
see the [[differences between prokaryotes and eukaryotes]]

the [[cell membrane]] is an effective barrier to separate the [[intracellular fluid]] and the [[extracellular fluid]]
