in biology, homeostasis is the state of steady internal physical and chemical conditions maintained by living systems

it maintains the optimal functioning conditions of the organism and includes many variables
- body temperature
- fluid balance
- pH of extracellular fluid
- concentrations of sodium, potassium and calcium ions
- blood sugar level

homeostasis is a natural resistance in change when already in optimal conditions, and equilibrium is maintained by many regulatory mechanisms
- negative feedback loop - when a variable changes, an opposite change directs it back to the equilibrium value
- stimulus-response model - stimulus noticed by receptor which uses the effector to cause a repsonse
