structurally speaking, ribonucleic acid, RNA, is quite similar to [[deoxyribonucleic acid|DNA]]
however, whereas DNA molecules are typically long and double stranded, RNA molecules are much shorter and single stranded
see the [[differences between DNA and RNA]]

RNA is coded with the bases
- adenine
- cytosine
- thymine
- uracil
