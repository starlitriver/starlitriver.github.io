polysaccharides are the most abundant [[carbohydrate|carbohydrates]] found in food

they are long-chain polymeric carbohydrates composed of more than 10 [[monosaccharide]] units

polysaccharides can have distinct properties from their monosaccharide monomers; they may be amorphous or insoluble in water

storage polysaccharides are common sources of energy
most organisms can break down starches into glucose

storage polysaccharides
- starch
- glycogen
- galactogen
- inulin

some structural polysaccharides such as cellulose cannot be metabolized by most organisms

structural polysaccharides
- cellulose
- chitin
- pectin
