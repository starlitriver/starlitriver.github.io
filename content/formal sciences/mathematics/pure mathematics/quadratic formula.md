quadratic is a fancy word for 2 degree polynomial - i'd rather they call it the 2 degree polynomial formula but doesn't sound as fancy :/

$x=\dfrac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}\text{ for }ax^2+bx+c=0$
 
 $b^2-4ac$ is called the [[discriminant]] and it can determine the number of solutions for the 2 degree polynomial
