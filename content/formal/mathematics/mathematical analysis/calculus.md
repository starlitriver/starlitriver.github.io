in [[formal/mathematics/mathematical analysis/index|mathematical analysis]], calculus is the study of continuous change

## differential calculus

differential calculus studies the rates at which variables change, where the instantaneous rate of change is calculated using the [[derivative]] of a function

see [[guide to differential calculus]]

## integral calculus

integral calculus studies [[integral|integrals]] in order to calculate the area under a curve, and related values
