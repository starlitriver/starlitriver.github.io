a 2 degree polynomial is also called a quadratic equation
the graph of a 2 degree polynomial is called a parabola

a 2 degree polynomial has a turning point - it is a point where the gradient is zero, and it is either the maximum or minimum point of the graph

#### standard form
$f(x)=ax^2+bx+c$
where a b c are constants
#### vertex form
$f(x)=a(x-h)^2+k$
where a h k are constants and (h,k) is the turning point of the equation
#### product form
$𝑓(𝑥) = 𝑎(𝑥 − 𝑥_1)(𝑥 − 𝑥_2)$
where a x_1 and x_2 are constants and the x values of the x-intercepts are x_1 and x_2.
