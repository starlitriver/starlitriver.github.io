also called a quartic equation

#### standard form

$f(x)=ax^4+bx^3+cx^2+dx+e$

#### turning point form

$f(x)=a(x-h)^4+k$

#### product form

$𝑓(𝑥) = 𝑎(𝑥 − 𝑥_1)(𝑥 − 𝑥_2)(𝑥 − 𝑥_3)(x-x_4)$
