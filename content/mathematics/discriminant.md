the discriminant of a polynomial is different for different degrees.

## 2 degree polynomial

the discriminant for a [[2 degree polynomial]] is $b^2-4ac\text{ for }ax^2+bx+c$
if discriminant $>0$ then there are 2 real solutions
if discriminant $=0$ then there is 1 real solution
if discriminant $<0$ then there are no real solutions
