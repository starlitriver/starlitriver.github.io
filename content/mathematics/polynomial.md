a polynomial is a math expression that has variables and only the operations addition, multiplication and exponentiation to positive integer powers, and has a finite number of terms

a polynomial with one term is a [[monomial]]
a polynomial with two terms is a [[binomial]]
a polynomial with three terms is a [[trinomial]]
thankfully polynomials with more than three terms don't have special names

a polynomial's highest [[exponentiation|exponent]] is called the degree of the polynomial
- [[2 degree polynomial]]
- [[3 degree polynomial]]
- [[4 degree polynomial]]
