quadratic is a fancy word for 2 degree polynomial - but imagine calling it the 2 degree polynomial formula

if $ax^2+bx+c=0$, $x=\dfrac{-b\pm\sqrt{b^2-4ac}}{2a}$
 
 $b^2-4ac$ is called the [[discriminant]] and it can determine the number of solutions for the 2 degree polynomial
