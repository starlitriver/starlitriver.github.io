the surface area to volume ratio is depicted as the SA:V ratio

start with some small 3d volume, like a sphere
as you increase the volume, the volume increases by 3 degrees while the surface area increases by 2 degrees

so, there is a certain magic to finding the best size for a sphere, or other shapes, to have the most or least surface area to volume ratio
