$$\sin(a+b) = \sin(a)*\cos(b)+\sin(b)*\cos(a)$$

$$\sin(a-b) = \sin(a)*\cos(b)-\sin(b)*\cos(a)$$

$$\cos(a+b) = \cos(a)*\cos(b)-\sin(b)*\sin(a)$$

$$\cos(a-b) = \cos(a)*\cos(b)+\sin(b)*\sin(a)$$

$$\sin(2a) = 2*\sin(a)*\cos(a)$$

$$\cos(2a) = \cos^2(a)-\sin^2(a)$$

$$\sin^2(a)+\cos^2(a) = 1$$

$$\sin^2(a) = 1-\cos^2(a)$$

$$\cos(2a) = 2*\cos^2(a)-1$$

$$\cos(2a) = 1-2*\sin^2(a)$$

$$\cos^2(a) = \dfrac{1}{2}\left(1+\cos(2a)\right)$$

$$\sin^2(a) = \dfrac{1}{2}\left(1-\cos(2a)\right)$$
