1-- solve each of the following for $x$


- $ax+n=m$
$ax=m-n$
$x=\dfrac{m-n}{a}$

- $ax+b=bx$
$ax-bx=-b$
$x=\dfrac{-b}{a-b}$
$x=\dfrac{b}{b-a}$

- $\dfrac{ax}{b}+c=0$
$ax=-cb$
$x=\dfrac{-cb}{a}$

- $px=qx+5$
$x(p-q)=5$
$x=\dfrac{5}{p-q}$

- $mx+n=nx-m$
$mx-nx=-m-n$
$x(m-n)=-m-n$
$x=\dfrac{-m-n}{m-n}$
$x=\dfrac{m+n}{n-m}$

- $\dfrac{1}{x+a}=\dfrac{b}{x}$
$x=b(x+a)$
$x=bx+ba$
$x-bx=ba$
$x=\dfrac{ba}{1-b}$

- $\dfrac{b}{x-a}=\dfrac{2b}{x+a}$
$b(x+a)=2b(x-a)$
$bx+ba=2bx-2ba$
$2bx-bx=ba+2ba$
$x=\dfrac{ba+2ba}{b}$
$x=3a$

- $\dfrac{x}{m}+n=\dfrac{x}{n}+m$
$xn+mn^2=xm+m^2n$
$xn-xm=m^2n-mn^2$
$x=\dfrac{m^2n-mn^2}{n-m}$
$x=\dfrac{mn(m-n)}{n-m}$
$x=\dfrac{-mn(n-m)}{n-m}$
$x=-mn$

- $-b(ax+b)=a(bx-a)$
$-bax-b^2=abx-a^2$
$2abx=a^2-b^2$
$x=\dfrac{a^2-b^2}{2ab}$


- $p^2(1-x)-2pqx=q^2(1+x)$
$p^2-xp^2-2pqx=q^2+xq^2$
$xq^2+2pqx+xp^2=p^2-q^2$
$(x)(p+q)^2=(p+q)(p-q)$
$x=\dfrac{p-q}{p+q}$

- $\dfrac{x}{a}-1=\dfrac{x}{b}+2$
$bx-ab=ax+2ab$
$bx-ax=3ab$
$x=\dfrac{3ab}{b-a}$

- $\dfrac{x}{a-b}+\dfrac{2x}{a+b}=\dfrac{1}{a^2-b^2}$
$xa+xb+2x(a-b)=\dfrac{(a+b)(a-b)}{(a+b)(a-b)}$
$x(a+b+2a-2b)=1$
$x=\dfrac{1}{3a-b}$

- $\dfrac{p-qx}{t}+p=\dfrac{qx-t}{p}$
$p^2-pqx+p^2t=qtx-t^2$
$qtx+pqx=p^2t+q^2+t^2$
$x(qt+pq)=\dfrac{p^2t+q^2+t^2}{qt+pq}$

- $\dfrac{1}{x+a}+\dfrac{1}{x+2a}=\dfrac{2}{x+3a}$
$(x+2a)(x+3a)+(x+a)(x+3a)=2(x+a)(x+2a)$
$x^2+5ax+6a^2+x^2+4ax+3a^2=2x^2+6ax+4a^2$
$5ax+6a^2+4ax+3a^2=6ax+4a^2$
$3ax=-5a^2$
$x=\dfrac{-5a}{3}$

4-- solve each of the following pairs of equations for $x$ and $y$

- $\begin{align}&y=ax-b\\ &y=cx+a\end{align}$
$ax-b=cx+a$
$x(a-c)=a+b$
$x=\dfrac{a+b}{a-c}$

$y=a(\dfrac{a+b}{a-c})-b$
$y=\dfrac{a^2+ba-b(a-c)}{a-c}$
$y=\dfrac{a^2+bc}{a-c}$


- $\begin{align}&ax+y=c\\ &x+by=d\end{align}$
$x+b(c-ax)=d$
$x+bc-abx=d$
$x(1-ab)=d-bc$
$x=\dfrac{d-bc}{1-ab}$

$ax+y=c$
$ax+aby=ad$
$y-aby=c-ad$
$y=\dfrac{c-ad}{1-ab}$

- $\begin{align}&ax-by=a^2\\ &bx-ay=b^2\end{align}$
$a^2x-aby=a^3$
$b^2x-aby=b^3$
$a^2x-b^2x=a^3-b^3$
$x=\dfrac{a^3-b^3}{a^2-b^2}$
$x=\dfrac{(a-b)(a^2+ab+b^2)}{(a+b)(a-b)}$
$x=\dfrac{a^2+ab+b^2}{a+b}$

$abx-b^2y=a^2b$
$abx-a^2y=ab^2$
$-b^2y+a^2y=a^2b-ab^2$
$y=\dfrac{a^2b-ab^2}{a^2-b^2}$
$y=\dfrac{(ab)(a-b)}{(a+b)(a-b)}$
$y=\dfrac{ab}{a+b}$


- $\begin{align}&ax+by=t\\ &ax-by=s\end{align}$
$2by=t-s$
$y=\dfrac{t-s}{2b}$

$2ax=t+s$
$x=\dfrac{t+s}{2a}$

- $\begin{align}&y=abx+ac\\ &y=ax-bc\end{align}$
$by=abx-b^2c$
$y-by=ac+b^2c$
$y=\dfrac{ac+b^2c}{1-b}$

$abx+ac=ax-bc$
$x(ab-a)=-bc-ac$
$x=\dfrac{ac+bc}{a-ab}$

- $\begin{align}&ax+by=a^2+2ab-b^2\\ &bx+ay=a^2+b^2\end{align}$
$abx+b^2y=b(a^2+2ab-b^2)$
$abx+a^2y=a(a^2+b^2)$
$b^2y-a^2y=ba^2+2ab^2-b^3-(a^3+ab^2)$
$y(b^2-a^2)=a^2b+2ab^2-b^3-a^3-ab^2$
$y(b^2-a^2)=a^2b+ab^2-a^3-b^3$
$y=\dfrac{a^2b+ab^2-a^3-b^3}{b^2-a^2}$
$y=\dfrac{(ab)(a+b)-a^3-b^3}{(b+a)(b-a)}$
$y=\dfrac{(ab)(a+b)+(-1)(a+b)(a^2-ab+b^2)}{(b+a)(b-a)}$
$y=\dfrac{ab-a^2+ab-b^2}{(b-a)}$
$y=\dfrac{(-1)(a^2-2ab+b^2)}{(b-a)}$
$y=\dfrac{(-1)(a-b)(a-b)}{(b-a)}$
$y=a-b$

$bx+a(a-b)=a^2+b^2$
$bx+a^2-ab=a^2+b^2$
$bx=b^2+ab$
$x=a+b$

5-- for each of the following, eliminate $h$ and express $s$ in terms of $a$

- $\begin{align}&s=ah\\ &h=2a+1\end{align}$
$h=\dfrac{s}{a}$
$\dfrac{s}{a}=2a+1$
$s=2a^2+a$

- $\begin{align}&s=ah\\ &h=a(2+h)\end{align}$
$h=\dfrac{s}{a}$
$\dfrac{s}{a}=2a+\dfrac{as}{a}$
$\dfrac{s}{a}=2a+s$
$\dfrac{s-sa}{a}=2a$
$s(1-a)=2a^2$
$s=\dfrac{2a^2}{1-a}$

- $\begin{align}&s=h^2+ah\\ &h=3a^2\end{align}$
$s=9a^4+3a^3$


- $\begin{align}&as=a+h\\ &h+ah=1\end{align}$
$h=\dfrac{1}{1+a}$
$as=a+\dfrac{1}{1+a}$
$s=1+\dfrac{1}{x^2+a}$
$s=\dfrac{a^2+a+1}{a^2+a}$

- $\begin{align}&as=s+h\\ &ah=a+h\end{align}$
$h=\dfrac{a}{a-1}$
$as=s+\dfrac{a}{a-1}$
$as-s=\dfrac{a}{a-1}$
$s(a-1)=\dfrac{a}{a-1}$
$s=\dfrac{a}{(a-1)^2}$

- $\begin{align}&as=a+2h\\ &h=a-s\end{align}$
$h=\dfrac{as-a}{2}$
$\dfrac{as-a}{2}=a-s$
$as-a=2a-2s$
$as+2s=3a$
$s=\dfrac{3a}{a+2}$
