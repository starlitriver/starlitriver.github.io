cell division is the final part of the [[cell cycle]]

in eukaryotes, there are two distinct types of cell division
- [[mitosis]] produces two daughter cells that are genetically identical to the parent cell
- [[meiosis]] produces haploid gametes for sexual reproduction

for [[unicellular organisms]], cell division is equal to [[reproduction]] as a new organism is created
