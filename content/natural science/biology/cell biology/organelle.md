an organelle is a specialized [[cell|cellular]] part that has a specific function and is considered analogous to an organ, therefore called organelles.
well, not exactly defined as a cell's organ, but rather broadly defined as "a subcellular structure with one or more specific jobs to perform in the cell"

a list of some organelles:

- [[nucleolus]]
- [[endoplasmic reticulum]]
- [[Golgi apparatus]]
