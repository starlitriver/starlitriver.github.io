in molecular genetics, a gene is a sequence of [[nucleotide|nucleotides]] in DNA that is transcribed to produce a functional RNA

each gene provides information on how to make a particular product needed by the cell

a gene can be either protein-coding or non-coding
many genes encode protein products - they specify the sequence of amino acids used to build a particular protein
