an organism is a living being, no matter how big or small™

umm, the exact definition of what a organism is... unclear
but according to broad definitions, an organism has the following properties
- has a clear boundary where everything encased is part of itself
- consists of one or more [[cell|cells]]
- possesses genetic information in [[nucleic acid|nucleic acids]]

and, this broad definition encompasses all things that are related to life in the intuitive sense!

i don't really want to get into the messy systematic biology and all that taxonomy of ontology
you can use this open source software https://protege.stanford.edu or use this website thing https://bioportal.bioontology.org
