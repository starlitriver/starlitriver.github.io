in cell biology, a chloroplast is an [[organelle]] found in plant and algal cells responsible for the process of [[photosynthesis]]

a chloroplast is characterized by its two membranes and a high concentration of [[chlorophyll]], green pigments

to create glucose, chloroplasts harness the energy from sunlight to convert carbon dioxide and water into glucose, with the chemical reaction used in photosynthesis, 
$12H_O+6CO_{2} \to C_6H_{12}O_6+6O_2$
