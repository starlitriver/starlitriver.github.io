in cell biology, an organelle is a specialized [[cell|cellular]] part that has a specific function and is considered analogous to an organ

to be specific , an organelle is a sub-cellular structure with one or more specific jobs to perform in the cell

[[prokaryote|prokaryotes]] and [[eukaryote|eukaryotes]] have different organelles

major eukaryotic organelles

| organelle                 | main function                                                                                           | organisms        |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ---------------- |
| [[cell membrane]]         | separates the cell interior from the outside environment                                                | all eukaryotes   |
| [[cell wall]]             | has rigid structure composed of cellulose that provides shape to the cell and relieves osmotic pressure | plants, protists |
| [[chloroplast]]           | photosynthesis, traps energy from sunlight                                                              | plants, algae    |
| [[endoplasmic reticulum]] | rough ER for translation and folding of new proteins, smooth ER for expression of lipids                | all eukaryotes   |
| [[flagellum]]             | locomotion and sensory                                                                                  | some eukaryotes  |
| [[Golgi apparatus]]       | sorting, packaging, processing and modification of proteins                                             | all eukaryotes   |
| [[mitochondrion]]         | energy production from the oxidation of glucose and releasing ATP                                       | most eukaryotes  |
| [[nucleus]]               | DNA maintenance, controls all activities of the cell, RNA transcription                                 | all eukaryotes   |
| [[vacuole]]               | storage and transportation                                                                              | all eukaryotes   |
