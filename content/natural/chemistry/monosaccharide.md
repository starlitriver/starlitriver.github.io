in chemistry, monosaccharides are the simplest form of [[carbohydrate|carbohydrates]]

(a carbohydrate consists of one or more monosaccharides)

monosaccharides are usually colorless, water-soluble and crystalline shaped organic solids

list of monosaccharides
- glucose
- fructose
- galactose
