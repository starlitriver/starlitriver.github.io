## anti-advice

for some reason, advice that tells a person to do the opposite of what the advice really is seem to work well, effectively getting to the audience

for example, see [7 ways to maximize misery](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=LO1mTELoj6o)
look at the interesting reverse psychology there!

so look, anti-advice is not common and we almost never see it
but oftentimes, when we do make anti-advice, the goal becomes clear.

it's kinda like someone telling you to do something but you want to do the opposite... anti-advice
