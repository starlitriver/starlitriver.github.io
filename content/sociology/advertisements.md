an advertisement is defined as a notice or announcement in a public medium promoting something

to promote is to support or encourage. i guess, when we really think about it, advertisements are just normal things, anything that has a purpose to support or encourage


the idea of ads being manipulative is so scary. but it's true - the digital age caused targeted ads that were absolutely horrible...

why would an advertisement be horrible?
it's about money, and using whatever means possible to get money?

surely, most ads have the pure intention of getting money, and that is indeed the reason why some ads are horrible

maybe, we'll look at society through ads, and see the worst parts of the world
