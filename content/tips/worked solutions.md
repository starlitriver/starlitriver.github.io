### #limit #rationalization 

$\lim \limits_{x \to -3} \frac{\sqrt{4x+28}-4}{x+3}$

direct substitution no work
rationalization

$\lim \limits_{x \to -3} \frac{\sqrt{4x+28}-4}{x+3}\cdot\frac{\sqrt{4x+28}+4}{\sqrt{4x+28}+4}$

$\lim \limits_{x \to -3} \frac{(4x+28)-4^2}{(x+3)(\sqrt{4x+28}+4)}$

$\lim \limits_{x \to -3} \frac{4x+12}{(x+3)(\sqrt{4x+28}+4)}$

$\lim \limits_{x \to -3} \frac{4(x+3)}{(x+3)(\sqrt{4x+28}+4)}$

$\lim \limits_{x \to -3} \frac{4}{\sqrt{4x+28}+4}$ for $x\neq-3$

=1/2

### #limit #trig-identities 

$\lim \limits_{\theta \to -\frac{\pi}{4}} \frac{1+\sqrt{2}\sin(\theta)}{\cos(2\theta)}$

direct substitution no work
trig identities

$\cos(2\theta)=\cos^2(\theta)-\sin^2(\theta)=1-2\sin^2(\theta)=2\cos^2(\theta)-1$

$1-2\sin^2(\theta) = (1+\sqrt{2}\sin(\theta))(1-\sqrt{2}\sin(\theta))$

$\lim \limits_{\theta \to -\frac{\pi}{4}} \frac{1+\sqrt{2}\sin(\theta)}{(1+\sqrt{2}\sin(\theta))(1-\sqrt{2}\sin(\theta))}$

$\lim \limits_{\theta \to -\frac{\pi}{4}} \frac{1}{1-\sqrt{2}\sin(\theta)} \text{, }\theta \neq \frac{\pi}{4}$

=1/2

### #limit #trig-identities 

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\sin^2(2\theta)}{1-\sin^2(\theta)}$

direct substitution no work
trig identities

  $\sin^2(a)+\cos^2(a) = 1$
  $1-\sin^2(a)=\cos^2(a)$

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\sin^2(2\theta)}{\cos^2(\theta)}$

$\sin(2a) = 2\cdot\sin(a)\cdot\cos(a)$

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{2\cdot\sin(\theta)\cdot\cos(\theta)}{\cos^2(\theta)}$

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{4\cdot\sin^2(\theta)\cdot\cos^2(\theta)}{\cos^2(\theta)}$

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}4\cdot\sin^2(\theta)$

=4



### #limit #trig-identities 

$\lim\limits_{x\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{3\cos^2(x)}{2-2\sin(x)}$

direct substitution no work
trig identities

$\sin^2(a)+\cos^2(a) = 1$
$\cos^2(a)=1-\sin^2(a)$
  
$\lim\limits_{x\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{3(1-\sin^2(x))}{2-2\sin(x)}$

difference of squares

$\lim\limits_{x\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{3(1+\sin(x))(1-\sin(x))}{2-2\sin(x)}$

$\lim\limits_{x\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{3(1+\sin(x))(1-\sin(x))}{2(1-\sin(x))}$

$\lim\limits_{x\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{3(1+\sin(x))}{2}$

=3



### #limit #trig-identities 

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\tan^2(\theta)\cdot(1-\sin(\theta)$

direct substitution no work
trig identities

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\sin^2(\theta)\cdot(1-\sin(\theta)}{\cos^2(\theta)}$

  $\sin^2(a)+\cos^2(a) = 1$
  $\cos^2(a)=1-\sin^2(a)$

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\sin^2(\theta)\cdot(1-\sin(\theta)}{1-\sin^2(\theta)}$

difference of squares

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\sin^2(\theta)\cdot(1-\sin(\theta)}{(1+\sin(\theta)(1-\sin(\theta))}$

$\lim\limits_{\theta\to\dfrac{\pi}{2}}\dfrac{\sin^2(\theta)}{1+\sin(\theta)}$

=1/2

### #limit #trig-identities 

$\lim \limits_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\cot^2(x)}{1-\sin(x)}$

expand cotangent

$\lim \limits_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{\cos^2(x)}{\sin^2(x)(1-\sin(x))}$

apply pythagorean identity

$\lim \limits_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{(1-\sin^2(x))}{\sin^2(x)(1-\sin(x))}$

difference of squares

$\lim \limits_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{(1-\sin(x))(1+\sin(x))}{\sin^2(x)(1-\sin(x))}$

$\lim \limits_{x \to \frac{\pi}{2}} \dfrac{1+\sin(x)}{\sin^2(x)}$

$\dfrac{1+\sin(\frac{\pi}{2})}{\sin^2(\frac{\pi}{2})}$

=2
