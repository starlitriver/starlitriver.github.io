in cell biology, a ribosome is an [[organelle]] that is in charge of synthesizing proteins 

ribosomes are made up of a complex of ribosomal RNA (rRNA) and proteins

ribosomes are used for the process of translation, with the use of binding sites 

ribosomes exist as two separate subunits until they are attached to a binding site on the mRNA molecule when they join together
